D&D Character Comparison

Instructions

Welcome to our D&D character comparison application! This app will give you a quick glance at how your character might stack up against common D&D monsters while providing epic music (Click on the YouTube icon) to inspire the soul. Feel free to create different chracters to test out different combinations!

You will be presented with an array of options to create your base character stats. When you finish, press submit, and you will be directed to the results page that shows your stats, as well as the option to generate a random monster so you can compare the two. Enjoy!
